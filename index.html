Tropical Beaches

Discover Sri Lanka's Stunning Coastline

Experience the pristine golden sands and turquoise waters of Sri Lanka's most beautiful beaches. From the surfing paradise of Arugam Bay to the whale watching hub of Mirissa, our beach packages offer the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Our beach tours include:

  • Whale watching excursions in Mirissa
  • Surfing lessons in Arugam Bay
  • Snorkeling in the coral reefs of Trincomalee
  • Sunset boat rides in Unawatuna
  • Seafood dining experiences
Best Time: Nov-Apr Duration: 3-7 Days Difficulty: Easy Family Friendly

Hill Country Escape

Explore Sri Lanka's Scenic Highlands

Journey through the misty mountains and lush tea plantations of Sri Lanka's hill country. Experience the famous train ride to Ella, visit working tea factories, and enjoy breathtaking views from mountain peaks.

Our hill country tours include:

  • Scenic train journey from Kandy to Ella
  • Tea plantation visits and tastings
  • Hiking to Little Adam's Peak
  • Visiting waterfalls and botanical gardens
  • Staying in colonial-era bungalows
Best Time: Jan-Apr Duration: 4-6 Days Difficulty: Moderate Photography Hotspot

Cultural Heritage Trail

Discover Sri Lanka's Ancient Civilizations

Step back in time and explore the ancient kingdoms of Sri Lanka. From the magnificent rock fortress of Sigiriya to the sacred city of Anuradhapura, our cultural tours offer a deep dive into Sri Lanka's rich history and heritage.

Our cultural tours include:

  • Climbing Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Exploring ancient cities of Polonnaruwa and Anuradhapura
  • Visiting the Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Traditional cultural shows
  • Local village experiences
Best Time: Dec-Mar Duration: 5-7 Days Difficulty: Moderate UNESCO Heritage Sites

Wildlife Safari

Encounter Sri Lanka's Incredible Wildlife

Embark on an unforgettable safari adventure through Sri Lanka's national parks. Spot leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and a stunning variety of birds in their natural habitats.

Our wildlife safaris include:

  • Early morning and evening jeep safaris
  • Expert local guides and trackers
  • Visits to Yala, Udawalawe, and Wilpattu National Parks
  • Elephant transit center visits
  • Bird watching experiences
